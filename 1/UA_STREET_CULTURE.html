THE VISION

This archive is a tribute to the streets of Ukraine. From the early 2000s in Kyiv to the massive murals in Kharkiv and the technical burners in Odesa, our culture is built on bravery, style, and brotherhood. We track the legends, the new blood, and the festivals that keep the paint flowing.

VULUCHNYI STYLE

It's more than paint; it's a lifestyle. We cover everything from "Bombing" (fast, illegal pieces) to "Hall of Fame" productions that take days to complete.

COMMUNITY

Ukraine has one of the tightest graffiti communities in Europe. Festivals like Alarm prove that even in hard times, art never stops.

GRAFFITI SCHOOL

Basics: The Tag

Before you buy paint, master your tag in a notebook. Focus on letter spacing and flow. Your tag is your ID.

Can Control

Skinny Caps: For outlines and details.
Fat Caps: For filling large areas quickly.
Keep the can moving at a steady speed to avoid drips.

Pro Tip: The Cut

To make a line sharper, use the background color to "cut" back into the letter. This creates that professional, surgical look seen in BIOS or DINED pieces.

FESTIVALS

ALARM Festival

The biggest graffiti event in UA. Bringing together hundreds of writers for massive legal walls across different cities like Lviv, Kyiv, and Lutsk.

Black Circle

Focused on the underground and independent spirit, celebrating raw style and community connection.
