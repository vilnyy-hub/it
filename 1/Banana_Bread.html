🍌 Banana Bread

How to cook banana bread

Recipe Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mash 3 ripe bananas.
  3. Add eggs, sugar, and butter.
  4. Add flour and baking powder.
  5. Mix the batter well.
  6. Pour into a baking pan.
  7. Bake for 50–60 minutes.
  8. Let it cool and enjoy!
