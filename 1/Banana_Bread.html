🍌 Banana Bread

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
2. Mash 3 ripe bananas in a bowl.
3. Mix in 75g of melted butter and 100g of sugar.
4. Add 1 egg and a splash of vanilla extract.
5. Stir in 200g of flour and 1 tsp of baking soda.
6. Bake for 50-60 minutes. Enjoy!
